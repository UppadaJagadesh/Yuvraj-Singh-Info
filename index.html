Yuvraj Singh

Indian Cricketer

Yuvraj Singh is a former Indian international cricketer who played in all formats of the game. He was particularly known for his aggressive and elegant stroke play and match winning all-round performances for India. He is an all-rounder who bats left-handed in the middle order and bowls slow left-arm orthodox.
